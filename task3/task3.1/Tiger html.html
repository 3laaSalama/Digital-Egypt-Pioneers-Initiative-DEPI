Tiger

 Easily recognized by its coat of reddish-orange with dark stripes, the tiger
 is the largest wild cat in the world. The big cat's tail is three feet long.On
average the big cat weighs 450 pounds, about the same as eight ten-year-
old kids.It stands three feet tall with teeth four inches long and claws as
long as house keys.

COMMON NAME:Bengal Tiger

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Panthera tigris tigris

TYPE: Mammals

DIET: Carnivore

LIFE SPAN IN THE WILD: 8 to 10 years

Head and body: 5 to 6 feet; tail: 2 to 3 feet

WEIGHT: 240 to 500 pounds
